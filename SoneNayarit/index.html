About Us

  • Orisia Nova

    An abstract artist hailing from New Orleans, Louisiana, USA where she began her creative journey. Raised among artisans and musicians, free artistic expression has always been forefront in her life. Her dream fully took flight once moving to Nayarit, where the myth and magic of the culture provide endless fuel for her imagination. Her creative use of color and texture is directly sourced by the sea, sky, and jungle shores of her new home.

  • Dana

    Of Natural Design Concepts creates stunning handmade furniture and accessories from exotic hardwoods, that she then inlays with gemstones, glass and shell. Her dream began over ten years ago, but truly came to life when she made the move to Puerto Vallarta from Ottowa, Ontario and found inspiration in the exotic woods of Mexico. She focuses on sourcing her wood responsibly, purchasing ancient felled trees to give them new life through her craft.

  • KT

    Relocated to Mexico almost six years ago with her dog, selling off everything she owned and packing the rest in her car to begin what would be a life changing adventure. Her background is varied, ranging in industries from fashion to non-profit, but always with an emphasis on expanding her knowledge base in design and production, KT fills various roles at Soñé, acting as business manager, buyer and designing a line of modern jewelry using a variety of mediums and techniques.
